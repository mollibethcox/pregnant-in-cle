About


Pregnant in CLE started with a desire to provide pregnant women with access to information and resources, and by doing so, reduce pregnancy anxiety and empower women to feel confident in their decisions and prepared for parenthood.


This starts with knowing what options exist in Cleveland. Options like: which hospital to choose, what doctor(s), what kinds of classes, if I need a doula. While information is at our fingertips, it takes research and time. Pregnant in CLE will serve as a directory of pregnancy-centered organizations and businesses in Cleveland, providing up-to-date and easily accessible information.


While the directory is still in development, an update page is being constructed in response to COVID-19. Information regarding in-person and virtual visits, restrictions, and extra precautions are being posted daily. If you have any questions, or if you are a business and would like to add or change your information, please contact us.
